# Human Robot Systems (ROB 204)

I have served as a Teaching Assistant for ROB 204 since Fall 2023 at the University of Michigan. I teach a lab section comprised of 20 students, 
hold office hours, write setup and troubleshooting documentations, have contributed to exam questions, and grade assignments and exams.

Topics:  
– Human Perception and Cognition
– Designing for Human Fit, Sizing, and Strength  
– Human–Robot Communication Modalities  
– Impact of Situation Awareness and Trust on Automation Design  
– User Interface Design for Robotic Systems  
– Assessing Usefulness and Usability of a Robotic System  
– Socially Engaged Designs  

# Human Robot Systems (ENGR 100)

I served as a Teaching Assistant for the Robotics section of ENGR 100 (a class required to be taken by all freshmen in the college of Engineering). I led a lab 
section of 24 students, held office hours, graded assignments, and designed a lab project on Computer Vision using OpenCV and Arduino!

Topics:  
– Robot building and embedded programming skills  
– Robotic sensing, decision-making, and actuation  
– State-machine formulation  
– Design process with stakeholder involvement
